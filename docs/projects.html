Blackjack
For this project, I created a fully functioning game of Blackjack with a simulated deck of 52 cards. The user starts with 100 units of an unnamed currency and can bet on each of their hands in the game. To start off, the user places a bet, and then them and the dealer get dealt two cards. They then have the option to double down or if they were dealt a pair of the same value card, to split their hand. They have the option to hit or to stand and if they get closer to 21 than the dealer without busting, they get paid their bet.

Kao Game
For this project, I created a Suika-Like Game. The user controls colorful circles that have certain facial expressions on them. They drop the circles into a box on the screen, and they can roll and bounce around in it. Whenever 2 circles that match touch, they merge into a bigger circle. This has a set progression, each merge giving the user bigger and bigger circles. The game resets when the user has a circle be outside of the box for too long. This occurs when they run out of space in the box.

Breakout Rogue
For this project, I created a breakout game with a bit of a twist. The user starts by picking a difficulty. This will affect numerous things such as the kinds of enemies that spawn. They also pick a ball with which to play with. There is a ball that fires projectiles and a ball that does more damage on collision. Each level, the player must break through bricks in order to get to the enemies which fire projectiles. Once they kill all of the enemies, the top opens and by getting the ball to touch the top they advance to the next level. They then get to either heal or pick an upgrade for their chosen ball and move on to the next level.
